here is a cat from Chisinau

Hi! My name is Napoleon and I live in Chisinau, sometimes called Kishinev. I live near the center of the city. Some might call me a stray, but the girls who work at the Cafe on Strada Tighina take care of me. The girls there named me Napoleon and give me food and some milk. I like to lay on my couch and socialize with customers at the cafe, especially those who stay to work on their nice and warm laptops.
